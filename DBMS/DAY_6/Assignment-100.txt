Fetch the empno from EmpVehicle that share same vehcile:
SELECT EV1.EMPNO FROM EMPVEHICLE EV1, EMPVEHICLE EV2 WHERE EV1.VEHICLEID=EV2.VEHICLEID AND 
EV1.EMPNO<>EV2.EMPNO
GROUP BY EV1.EMPNO
